Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 9 March 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application (the "Application") developed and published by More Beans Developments (the "Service Provider"). The Application is provided as an ad-supported service and is intended for use "AS IS".

This policy explains what data is collected when you use the Application, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that data. By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not require you to create an account or register. The Service Provider does not directly collect personally identifiable information from you.

However, the Application uses third-party services that may collect certain data in order to serve advertisements and improve performance. The data collected by these third-party services may include:

  • Your device's IP address, which may be used to estimate approximate location
  • A device identifier such as your advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Advertising data, such as advertisements you have seen
  • Product interaction data, such as app launch taps and video views
  • Performance data, such as app launch time and energy usage
  • Non-user related crash logs and diagnostic information

This data is collected and processed by our third-party advertising partner. Please review their privacy policy for full details:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

Advertising

The Application uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements, including rewarded ads that allow you to unlock additional features. Google and its partners may use your device identifier and other data to serve personalised advertisements based on your prior interactions with this Application and other apps or websites.

You may opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Via Google Ads Settings
  • Via your device settings under Privacy > Tracking (iOS)

If you opt out of personalised advertising, you will still see advertisements in the Application, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

Location Information

The Application does not request or collect precise real-time location data from your device. An approximate location may be inferred from your IP address by our third-party advertising partner for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements. This is handled entirely by the third-party service and not by the Service Provider directly.

Children's Privacy

The Application is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. However, the Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If it is discovered that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be deleted immediately from our records.

If you are under the age of 16 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), you should obtain permission from a parent or guardian before using the Application.

Your Rights (UK & EEA Users)

If you are located in the United Kingdom or the European Economic Area, you have rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR respectively. These include the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority (in the UK, the ICO at ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below.

Your Rights (California Users — CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed, and to whom
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

The Application does not sell your personal information directly. However, the sharing of device identifiers and advertising data with Google AdMob may constitute a "sale" under the CCPA's broad definition. You may opt out via your device's ad personalisation settings.

Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting your information. The Application does not store personal data on our own servers. All data handling is performed by our third-party partners in accordance with their own security practices. While we endeavour to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.
